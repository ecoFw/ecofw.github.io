title: “Readings for Class”

These readings are meant to compliment and further stimulate your thoughts on the topics covered in class. Generally, they cover three topics:

  1. A chef’s perspective on ecological foodways (e.g. Barber, Nillson, Sherman)
  2. The neurology and physiology of flavor (e.g. Shepherd, Stuckey)
  3. Ways to observe ecological foodways (e.g. Kurlansky, Jacobson, Meadows)

If you are using a mobile device (e.g., tablet, phone, e-reader), you may need to hold the links and select “Open in a New Tab”.
